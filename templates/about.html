About AI Resume Fixer

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower job seekers worldwide by providing a free, powerful, and easy-to-use tool to instantly improve their resumes. We believe that a great resume should not be a barrier to landing your dream job.

How It Works

Using advanced AI from Perplexity, our tool analyzes the resume text you provide to correct grammar, optimize it for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) used by recruiters, and add measurable, high-impact statements to your achievements. The entire process is designed to be quick, intuitive, and effective.

Our Commitment to You

This tool is, and will always be, completely free to use. We are committed to helping you put your best foot forward in your career journey.
