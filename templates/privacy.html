Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AI Resume Fixer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

When you use our service, you provide us with resume text by either pasting it into a text box or uploading a file. We only collect the text content you provide for the purpose of processing it.

How We Use Information

The resume text you provide is sent directly to a third-party API (Perplexity AI) to be processed. The AI improves the text and sends it back to be displayed on our website. We do not store your resume text on our servers. The data is processed in real-time and is not retained by our application.

Third-Party Services

- Perplexity AI: We use the Perplexity API to process your resume. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policy.
- Google AdSense: We plan to use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.
